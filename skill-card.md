## Description: <br>
Short Drama Writer helps agents generate Chinese vertical short-drama outlines, character sheets, scenes, hooks, twists, and related writing guidance. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ckchzh](https://clawhub.ai/user/ckchzh) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External creators, writers, and agents use this skill to draft short-form drama story structures, character concepts, episode outlines, hooks, twists, and production-oriented writing prompts for vertical video dramas. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: One bundled script saves command inputs to a local history.log file under the skill data directory. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid entering confidential story ideas, client material, secrets, or unreleased plans when using the local CLI, or configure and review the data directory before use. <br>
